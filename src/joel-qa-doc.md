## Why He Moved from Security into Product Engineering

Joel loved the context his security role gave him — seeing how to build high-quality, innovative products while managing risk. Over time he realized he was more excited by the building than the risk management side, so he made the deliberate move toward full-stack and product engineering. He still carries that security-first mindset into everything he builds.
